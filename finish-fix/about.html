Our mission is to provide quality, exterior automotive repairs at wholesale pricing to everyone.

Finish Fix was founded on the idea that you don't have to sacrifice quality for price when it comes to exterior vehicle repairs. We use high quality materials and established repair best practices to provide quality, mobile services to everyone.

Meet Our Team

Our founding

Finish Fix was founded in 2023 by Tyler Clarkson, an experieced body repair and paint technician with over 10 years of relevant experience. He believes that quality comes first, and that affordable pricing is one of the best ways to provide value.

Growth & beyond

Finish Fix operates primarily on a scheduled basis with existing/new accounts; we will set up an agreed upon time and frequency to dedicate time and resources to take care of your needs. Our focus is on maintaining and servicing the needs of existing accounts, while growing to provide excellent service to all of San Antonio.
